OUR STORY

We're on a mission to make sure you never pay full price for the gear you love.

WildDeals is an independent, Canadian-built project — a weekly newsletter and mobile app that hunts down the best deals on camping, fishing, hiking, hunting and outdoor gear from every retailer worth watching. Currently tracking Canada & USA, with Europe coming next.

Why WildDeals exists

Outdoor gear is expensive. Really expensive. A decent tent runs $400, a pair of hiking boots runs $300, and by the time you've kitted out a weekend camping trip you're looking at four figures — before you've even filled the cooler.

The deals are out there. Retailers run major sales every week. The problem is nobody has time to check 15 websites every morning to find them. So we built a tool that does the hunting for you.

WildDeals started as a spreadsheet for personal use, turned into a weekly email for friends, and is now becoming a full mobile app with real-time deal alerts — so you'll know the moment that tent you've been eyeing drops 40%.

What we stand for

No paywalls. Ever. The app is 100% free. The weekly newsletter is free. We make money through affiliate links — never by charging you for access to deals.

Honesty over hype. We only share deals we'd buy ourselves, and we'll always tell you when a "sale" is fake or when a cheaper version is actually better.

Support the outdoors. We believe the outdoors should be accessible to everyone, and saving money on gear is a huge part of that.
