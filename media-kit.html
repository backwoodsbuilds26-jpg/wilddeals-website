About WildDeals

The Best Deals in the Wild

WildDeals is a Canadian mobile app that aggregates the best outdoor gear deals from Amazon.ca and hundreds of other retailers. We help outdoor enthusiasts find deep discounts on hunting, fishing, camping, hiking, and adventure gear — updated daily so users never miss a sale.

Our audience is passionate about the outdoors. They are active buyers who research gear, compare prices, and act quickly on deals. WildDeals puts the right products in front of the right audience at the right time.

Partnership Opportunity

Affiliate & Sponsorship

WildDeals drives high-intent traffic from Canadian outdoor enthusiasts directly to retailer product pages. Our users are actively looking to buy — they've already decided they want the gear, they're just looking for the best price.

Affiliate Integration

We support Amazon Associates, SkimLinks, ShareASale, CJ Affiliate, and direct affiliate programs. Integration is seamless — we link directly to your product pages with proper attribution.

Sponsored Deals

Feature your products as promoted deals within the app. Sponsored placements appear at the top of relevant categories and reach users who are actively browsing for discounts.

Brand Partnerships

Run exclusive flash sales, bundle deals, or seasonal promotions through WildDeals. We'll feature your brand with push notifications to subscribed users in your category.

Data & Insights

Understand what Canadian outdoor enthusiasts are searching for, saving, and buying. We provide anonymized trend reports and category insights to help optimize your promotions.
